My Journey

Seeing loved ones suffered from illness made me realize the importance of taking care of our body. But how can we do to stay healthy? I noticed that food plays a key role in our health since it provides energy and nutrients for our body to work. That’s how I started my nutrition journey.

After years of training and practice in nutrition and dietetics, my experience reinforces my belief that nutrition is one of the fundamentals of wellness. However, I also recognize that we generally focus on physical health but forget our mind and soul. Food provides fuel and nutrients to meet our needs- physically, mentally, emotionally, spiritually and socially.

Learning from Intuitive Eating and Health at Every Size® makes me believe that we need to have a positive relationship with food and body before digging deeper into nutrition. Therefore, I decide to help more people make peace with food and take care of their own body, mind, and soul.

My Philosophy



Foods should not be considered as “good” or “bad”

“Chocolate is bad for you.” “Vegetables are healthy.” You may find this sound familiar. It is not uncommon to see foods being labeled this way. However, labeling foods as “good” vs. “bad” is not helping with our health but in fact ruining our relationship with food. There is not “right” or “wrong” food. Balance, variety and moderation are the keys to make all foods fit!


We can trust our inner wisdom when making choices around food

We are the expert of our own life! We know ourselves better than others do; therefore, we don’t need people to make rules for us to follow. Instead of looking for external rules, we can reconnect to our internal voice and let it guide us to decide what to eat, when to eat and how much to eat.


Health cannot be determined by weight

Weight and body shapes cannot be used to measure a person’s health, beauty, or worth. Weight stigma is a crucial issue in our culture and only brings negative effects on human’s overall health. Our bodies hold us every single day and do lots of hard work for us. It deserves to be loved and respected, regardless of size.


We all can enjoy the satisfaction and happiness of eating

Foods provides fuel and nutrients for our body, mind, and soul. Foods are our friends rather than enemies. Foods are supposed to bring us joy and satisfaction, not guilt and shame. We can enjoy eating by focusing on the smell, taste, and texture of food. This helps us to maintain a peaceful relationship with food and to be free from guilt and shame.

My Life

While food and nutrition are a big part of both my work and personal life, I make sure that I save some time for different matters. During my downtime, I enjoy traveling around to meet people from other countries, to learn more about different cultures, and to appreciate the beauty of the world. I also like to refresh my mind by working out, doing yoga, reading and even just window shopping!

I give myself time and space to add some self-care to my daily routine and to take care of my body, mind and soul. This is my secret of keeping me nourished!

心 營養 | Make peace with food

一個幫助你與飲食建立良好關係的頻道

飲食應該要能同時維持生理和心理上的健康，但往往我們只注重飲食對於生理上的影響，而忽略了心理上的需求。

我們時常盲目地追隨著媒體、網路的建議來調整自己的飲食，可是卻很少聆聽自己身體的聲音，聽聽身體告訴我們，他需要什麼。

我們經常不自覺地被囚禁在社會對於外表、身型的刻板印象中，讓自己的飲食行為亂了序，讓身體承受著生理和心理的壓力。

其實，任何食物都可以是健康的食物，各種身材都值得被接受和愛護。飲食應該帶來滿足和快樂，而不是愧疚和罪惡。心 營養想和你一起，跟飲食建立良好的關係，和食物和睦相處，讓我們重新愛護自己。

在心 營養裡，營養師雅雯會帶你從心開始，重新認識飲食和營養。
